Round Light Object "Japanese Paper"

The lamp "Japanpapier" is covered with an original Japanese soy paper. Sojipaptes are traditionally used in Japan for covering sliding doors and are also very well suited for luminaire construction because of their translucence. This soy paper is natural white and has a very nice hemp water droplet structure. The interior is covered with over 500 individual RGB LEDs on tapes and is therefore very evenly illuminated, so that the individual water drops and hemp fibers are very effective. The light can be dimmed via a remote control and controlled via 16 different colors. The outer frame was worked ("gilded") with several layers of copper (impact metal) and connected to the soy paper by a small copper edge on the front side.

    Round Light Object "Japanese Paper":

  • Handmade single piece
  • Frame: Wood frame with multilayer sheet metal refinement in copper, clear lacquer seal
  • Diameter: 90 cm, height: 7 cm
  • Cover: Japanese paper - shoji paper with PVC coating, natural white with hemp fiber water droplet structure
  • Lighting: network powered RGB LEDs (16 colors, color changing function, dimmable), incl. remote control, power supply 12V, white cable
  • Price: 480 €

Round Light Object "Water"

The "water" light object has a water close-up motif with different shades of blue and single yellow tones. The motif is is applied with a foil to a round acrylic glass plate. The luminaire is internally lined with white LEDs on ribbons and is thereby illuminated very evenly so that the individual water drops and hemp fibers are very effective. The light can be controlled and dimmed via a remote control. The outer frame was treated with several layers of copper (impact metal) ("gilded"). The copper was oxidized in several steps and worked with varnish so that a very beautiful turquoise on the copper was created, which connects nicely with the picture.

    Round Light Object "Water":

  • Handmade single piece
  • Frame: Wood frame with multilayer sheet metal refinement in copper, clear lacquer seal, oxidized
  • Diameter: 90 cm, height: 7 cm
  • Cover: Photoprint on acrylic glass
  • Lighting: network powered white LEDs (dimmable), incl. Remote control, power supply 12V, white cable
  • Price: 580 €

Round Light Object "Skycrystal NYC"

Die runde Leuchte " Skycrystal NYC" mit einem Durchmesser von 88 cm und einem bemalten Außenrahmen wird von innen (weiß) beleuchtet.

Das Motiv der Leuchte "Skycrystal NYC" ist eine Nahaufnahme einer dicken, strukturierten Glasfront, die hoch über den Dächern von New York City aufgenommen wurde.

Auf dem runden Außenrahmen sind mehrere Schichten Weiß, Eisen- und Rostfarben aufgetragen, die ineinander zerfließen und sich in den Farben des Bildes wiederfinden.

Das Motiv ist mittels einer Folie auf einer Acrylglasplatte aufgebracht und mit Hilfe von weißen LED-Modulen ausgeleuchtet.

Durch die Beleuchtung gewinnen die Glasstrukturen und Farben zusätzlich an Tiefe und Glanz.

Das Licht kann über eine Fernbedienung gesteuert und gedimmt werden.

    Round Light Object "Skycrystal NYC":

  • Handmade single piece
  • Frame: Wood frame with multilayer sheet metal refinement in copper, clear lacquer seal, oxidized
  • Diameter: 90 cm, height: 7 cm
  • Cover: Photoprint on acrylic glass
  • Lighting: network powered white LEDs (dimmable), incl. Remote control, power supply 12V, white cable
  • Price: 580 €
